WinBitTorrent bundled backend — corresponding source offer
==========================================================

The bundled qbittorrent-nox.exe is built without source modifications from the
official qBittorrent tag release-5.2.3. qBittorrent is licensed under
GPL-2.0-or-later for source and GPL-3.0-or-later for this binary distribution,
with the OpenSSL exception stated in its COPYING file.

The complete build recipe, exact source URLs, SHA-256 values, vcpkg registry
baseline and dependency versions are in build/build-backend.ps1 and
build/vcpkg.json. Those files are the durable written offer for obtaining the
corresponding source used by this distribution. The exact upstream tag is:

  https://github.com/qbittorrent/qBittorrent/tree/release-5.2.3

The libtorrent source is the official v2.0.13 tag. Qt 6.11.1, Boost 1.91.0,
OpenSSL 3.6.2 and their transitive source archives are resolved by the pinned
vcpkg baseline. CPython 3.13.14 is distributed under the Python Software
Foundation License.

This offer is valid for at least three years after the last binary distribution
of this WinBitTorrent release. A complete source archive can also be produced by
running the build script; downloaded, hash-verified source archives remain under
build/.work/downloads.
